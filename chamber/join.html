Membership Benefits

Non-Profit

  • Free membership with basic listing in the chamber directory.
  • Access to networking events and workshops.
  • Volunteer opportunities for community involvement.

Bronze

  • Enhanced listing in the chamber directory with logo inclusion.
  • Invitation to exclusive member-only events.
  • Discounted rates for chamber-sponsored training programs.
  • Recognition on the chamber's social media platforms.

Silver

  • Priority placement in the chamber directory and website.
  • Featured spotlight on the chamber's homepage.
  • Complimentary attendance to select chamber events.
  • Access to member-to-member discounts and offers.

Gold

  • Premier placement in the chamber directory, website, and promotional materials.
  • Exclusive sponsorship opportunities for chamber events.
  • Priority access to chamber leadership and resources.
  • Customized marketing and advertising opportunities.
  • VIP invitations to high-profile chamber functions and receptions.
