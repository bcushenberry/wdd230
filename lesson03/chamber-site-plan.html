Chamber of Commerce Site Design

Brandon Cushenberry

Site Name

San Juan Capistrano Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The Chamber of Commerce for San Juan Capistrano aims to introduce and promote local businesses to the greater population, and to educate business owners through workshops and seminars to help chamber members grow their business.

Scenarios

  • - What is the schedule for upcoming training and educational events?
  • - What sort of support can I receive for hosting events to gain more exposure?
  • - What sort of promotions or deals are available to chamber members?

Color Schema

Earthy colors will be used to reflect the earthy feel of the town's buildings and scenery.

  • - #283618: This dark greenish color will be used for the background of the header, footer, and potentially other sections
  • - #FEFAE0: This light color will be used for text.
  • - #BC6C25: This may be used for highlights and/or h-level text.
  • - #606C38 and #DDA15E may also be used.

This list is by no means final, but simply some ideas.

Typography

  • - Montserrat: This font will be the main font for the site
  • - Karla: This font may be used, or it may be replaced with a different font
